Reconnect

Craft is not merely a livelihood skill, it is a meaningful network of knowledge, customs and social relationships. The stronger the existing network the better is the capability of the community to adapt. The Goh-Bugun project reconnects the community

  • With traditional knowledge through a network of makers across Bugun villages
  • With the outside world through craft as their cultural identity
  • With local materials, geography, and the floral bio diversity
